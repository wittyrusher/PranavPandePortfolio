About Me

"A designer at heart — shaping stories through clean, human-first interfaces."

Hi, I'm Pranav Mukunda Pande — a builder of both experiences and systems. I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications at Vishwakarma University (Class of 2026), after completing my BCA from MES Garware College in 2024.

I'm the founder of Atlas Hoppers, a travel venture born out of my love for discovery and logistics. Over the past few years, I've planned and led 100+ successful group tours — school trips, college IVs, family vacations, and more — across destinations like Manali, Jaipur, Kerala, Jim Corbett, Gujarat, and local gems like Kolad and Mahabaleshwar.

Alongside my journey in travel, I also build websites. I specialize in frontend development, creating clean, responsive, and purpose-driven websites — especially for travel, educational, and social impact platforms. I designed and developed the Atlas Hoppers website myself, and I love turning ideas into interactive digital spaces.

I love challenges, believe in learning by doing, and thrive when I'm solving real-world problems — whether on the road or on the web.
